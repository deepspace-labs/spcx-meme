The joke

We have 1,000,000,000 SPCX tokens. The world population is around 8,000,000,000 people.

"If we shared them out across the entire planet, everyone would get 1,000 SPCX each." 🤔

Sound familiar? It is the same creative arithmetic as the classic meme:

"If a certain billionaire shared his fortune with all of humanity, we would all be rich."

In reality it would be around 50 € each. And in our case it would be 0.125 SPCX each. That is exactly the joke.

About the logo

The SPCX logo, as you can see at the top of this page, is a completely black square.

This is intentional. It is not a missing image. It is not a placeholder. It is deep space — the cold, dark, mostly empty cosmic vacuum where rockets do their work.

The rocket is in there. You just can't see it. Because it is dark. Because it is space.
