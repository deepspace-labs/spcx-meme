## The joke

We have **1,000,000,000** SPCX tokens. The world population is around **8,000,000,000** people.

> *"If we shared them out across the entire planet, everyone would get 1,000 SPCX each."* 🤔

Sound familiar? It is the same creative arithmetic as the classic meme:

> *"If **a certain billionaire** shared his fortune with all of humanity, we would all be rich."*

(In reality it would be around 50 € each. And in our case it would be 0.125 SPCX each. But that's exactly the joke.)

**That is SPCX.** A tribute to badly-done division, to memes about reusable rockets, and to a certain company that might soon go public on the stock market. No, we will not say which one.
