## 4. Risks (non-exhaustive)

Holding or transacting in SPCX exposes you to risks including but not limited to:

### 4.1 Total loss of value
The token may, at any time, drop to a price effectively indistinguishable from zero. There is no floor, no guarantee, no buyback, no insurance fund.

### 4.2 Liquidity risk
SPCX's liquidity is provided by a single Raydium CPMM pool. As of the most recent update:

- The creator wallet **holds the LP tokens** (they are not burned).
- This means the creator has the technical ability to withdraw liquidity from the pool.
- The creator does not plan to do this, but **on-chain reality is the only guarantee**: until LP tokens are burned, the liquidity is removable in principle.

If liquidity is removed by any means, the practical ability to sell SPCX may disappear.
